OppSeek - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2025

Introduction

OppSeek ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

OppSeek collects and stores the following information:

  • Anonymous User IDs: Generated automatically via Firebase Anonymous Authentication
  • Connection Codes: Unique 6-character codes used to connect with friends
  • Job URLs and Titles: Information about job postings you choose to share
  • Optional Notes: Any notes you add when sharing jobs with friends
  • Timestamps: When jobs are shared and when connections are made

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Enable job sharing between you and your connected friends
  • Provide notifications when friends share jobs with you
  • Maintain your friend connections
  • Display your shared jobs history

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely in Google Firebase with appropriate security rules
  • Your data is protected by Firebase's enterprise-grade security
  • Only you and friends you've explicitly connected with can see your shared jobs
  • We implement authentication and access controls to protect your information

Data Sharing

  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers or marketers
  • Your shared jobs are only visible to friends you've connected with using connection codes
  • We use Firebase (Google Cloud) as our backend infrastructure provider

Your Rights and Controls

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect from friends at any time
  • Delete shared jobs from your history
  • Stop using the extension at any time by uninstalling it
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us

Third-Party Services

Job Share uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, and cloud storage. View Firebase's privacy policy at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Children's Privacy

Job Share is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the extension. If you uninstall the extension and do not use it for 90 days, you may request data deletion by contacting us.

Compliance

Job Share complies with applicable data protection laws and Chrome Web Store policies.
